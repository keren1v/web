1. What is Money?

Money is a tool we use to exchange goods and services. It can be coins, bills, or digital currency. Understanding money helps us make smart financial decisions.

  • Used to buy goods and services
  • Represents value
  • Helps store and measure wealth
  • Essential for trade and economy

2. Why Money Matters

Money affects many areas of our lives. Knowing how to manage it is key to achieving goals and security.

  • Helps buy what we need and want
  • Provides security for the future
  • Allows investment in education or projects
  • Enables planning for emergencies

3. How Money Works

Money has different forms and functions. Understanding this helps manage it wisely.

Key Points

  • Cash, digital payments, and bank accounts
  • Value changes over time (inflation)
  • Used for saving, spending, and investing
  • Needs to be managed responsibly
