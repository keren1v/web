1. Why Saving Matters

Saving money helps us prepare for the future and unexpected expenses. It allows us to reach goals without financial stress.

  • Provides financial security
  • Helps reach short-term and long-term goals
  • Reduces stress and uncertainty
  • Encourages smart spending habits

2. Budgeting Basics

Creating a budget helps you track income and expenses, so you can make informed financial choices.

  • List all income sources 💰
  • Track all expenses 📝
  • Identify unnecessary spending
  • Allocate money for savings

3. Tips for Saving Money

Simple habits make saving easier and more effective.

Tips

  • Save a fixed percentage of income
  • Set financial goals
  • Use savings accounts or digital wallets
  • Reduce unnecessary spending
