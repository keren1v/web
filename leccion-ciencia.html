Water is very special, it can change its form like a chameleon! Discover how it looks in its three states.

Solid: Ice

Water freezes with the cold and turns into ice or snow. It is strong and hard!

Liquid: Water

It's the form we know best, the one we drink. It flows and adapts to any shape!

Gas: Vapor

When it gets very hot, it turns into an invisible gas that rises into the sky. It's very light!
