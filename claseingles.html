Lesson: Personal Pronouns

A pronoun is a word that replaces a noun. They help us avoid repeating names and make our sentences sound more natural. Let's learn them! ðŸ‘‡

The Personal Pronouns

They are divided into **Subject** and **Object** pronouns. Subject pronouns perform the action, and object pronouns receive the action.

Subject Object
I me
you you
he him
she her
it it
we us
they them

Examples in a sentence

Subject: She is a good student.

Object: The teacher helps her.

Subject: They are playing soccer.

Object: I saw them at the park.

Subject: We love to learn English.

Object: The teacher teaches us.
