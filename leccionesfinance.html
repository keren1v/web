1. Understanding Financial Education

Financial education helps you make better decisions about your money. It covers earning, spending, saving, and planning.

  • Know where your money comes from and where it goes
  • Understand needs vs. wants
  • Learn how to avoid debt and save wisely

Reflection Activity

Write down two money habits you want to improve and one financial goal you have.

2. Personal Budget Basics

A personal budget helps you plan your income and expenses. It shows how much you earn and what you spend each month.

  • Income: salary, side jobs, government help
  • Fixed expenses: rent, electricity, water
  • Variable expenses: food, transport, leisure
  • Savings: set aside money regularly

Try This

Make a simple list of your monthly income and expenses. Is there anything you can adjust or reduce?

3. Saving and Spending Smart

Good saving habits give you peace and protection. Try these tips to stretch your money further:

  • Use envelopes or apps to organize spending
  • Look for discounts and buy only what you need
  • Set short- and long-term savings goals

Goal Setting

Think about something you want to save for: emergency fund, a device, or a trip. How much would you need?
