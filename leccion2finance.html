1. Why Save Money?

Saving money gives you financial security, prepares you for emergencies, and helps you reach future goals.

  • Build an emergency fund
  • Plan for future purchases
  • Reduce financial stress

Simple strategies can help you save consistently and effectively.

  • Set automatic transfers to savings
  • Cut unnecessary expenses
  • Track your spending regularly

3. Introduction to Investments

Investing helps your money grow over time through returns and compounding interest.

  1. Stocks: Ownership in companies with potential for high returns.
  2. Bonds: Safer investments with fixed interest.
  3. Mutual Funds: Diversified portfolios managed by professionals.
  4. Real Estate: Property investments for rental or resale value.
