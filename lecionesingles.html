1. Learn Basic Greetings

In English, greetings are used to say hello and to start conversations. Let's begin with the most common expressions.

  • Hello
  • Hi
  • Good morning / afternoon / evening
  • How are you?
  • Nice to meet you

2. How to Introduce Yourself

Introducing yourself is useful in workplaces, classes, and social situations. Try using these sentences:

  • My name is [your name].
  • I am from [your country or city].
  • I work as a [your profession].
  • I like [your interest or hobby].

Practice Activity

Fill in the blanks:
Hi! My name is ______. I'm from ______. I work as a ______. Nice to meet you!

3. Formal and Informal Greetings

Use formal greetings in professional or polite settings. Informal greetings are okay with friends and family.

Examples

  • Formal: Good morning. How do you do?
  • Informal: Hey! What's up?

4. Cultural Tips About Greetings

Different cultures greet differently. In English-speaking countries, handshakes are common. Some people may wave or nod.
