Story of the Day: The Forest Wizard 🌳

Deep within an enchanted forest, lived an old wizard named Elio. Elio didn't use magic wands; he created his magic with words. Every morning, he wrote in a giant book that glowed with its own light. The words he wrote turned into forest creatures: if he wrote "butterfly," a butterfly with bright colors flew off the page. If he wrote "river," a small stream began to flow between the trees. The forest animals loved him very much, because Elio always wrote words of kindness and joy, making the forest a happy place for everyone.
